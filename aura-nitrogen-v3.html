Resin
Division

UF and MUF amino resins for engineered wood panel manufacturing, agricultural binders, foam insulation, and corrugated board production.

Division Overview

Amino Resins
for Industry

Amino resins are condensation products of amino compounds (urea, melamine) with formaldehyde. Our resin division manufactures UF and MUF thermosetting resins — the most cost-effective bonding system for engineered wood panel production.

  • UF resins for plywood, particle board, OSB, and MDF
  • MUF resins for moisture-resistant and exterior-grade panels
  • Agricultural resins for slow-release fertilizer coating
  • Animal feed pellet binding agents
  • Cost-effective UF foam insulation systems
  • Corrugated cardboard resins with enhanced water and crush resistance

Chemical

UFC 85 — Urea Formaldehyde Concentrate. The highest commercially available concentration for industrial and agricultural chemical processes.

UFC 85

Urea Formaldehyde
Concentrate

UFC 85 is a clear viscous liquid composed of approximately 60% formaldehyde, 25% urea, and 15% water. It is the highest commercially available concentration of formaldehyde — noncombustible, highly stable, and requiring no heating for storage.

With a shelf life of up to two years, UFC 85 is the most user-friendly commercially available form of formaldehyde, making it the industry standard for fertilizer manufacturers and amino resin producers worldwide.

  • Anti-caking agent for urea granules in fertilizer production
  • Manufacture of liquid and dry nitrogen fertilizers
  • Manufacture of amino resins (UF and MUF)
  • Permanent press resins for textiles
  • Manufacture of moulding compounds
  • Industrial chemical process applications

Global
Partners

Authorised distribution partnerships with three world-class industrial manufacturers.

H.B.
Fuller
Industrial Adhesives Partner

H.B. Fuller Company is one of the world's largest adhesive manufacturers, with operations in over 35 countries. Their industrial adhesives, sealants, and chemical products serve packaging, hygiene, building, transportation, electronics, and manufacturing markets globally.

Through our distribution partnership, Aura Nitrogen supplies H.B. Fuller products — including Advantra hotmelt and specialty industrial adhesive systems — to clients in the Caribbean for packaging, construction, and manufacturing applications.

Jowat
Hotmelt & Reactive Adhesives Partner

Jowat SE is a German family-owned adhesive manufacturer with over a century of expertise in hotmelt, reactive polyurethane, water-based, and solvent adhesive technologies. Jowat products serve woodworking, packaging, paper processing, and bookbinding industries worldwide.

Their Jowatherm polyolefin hotmelt systems and reactive PU adhesives are available through Aura Nitrogen for woodworking panel manufacturers, packaging facilities, and aseptic packaging operations including Tetra Pak applications.

Sika
Construction Chemicals Partner

Sika AG is a Swiss global specialty chemicals company with a leading position in products for bonding, sealing, damping, reinforcing, and protecting in the building sector and motor vehicle industry, operating in more than 100 countries.

Sika products distributed by Aura Nitrogen — including sealants, construction adhesives, and waterproofing systems — serve building contractors, civil engineers, and industrial manufacturers across the Caribbean region.
